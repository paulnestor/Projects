Options of Nicotine Replacement Therapy (releasing of nicotine into your body to help ease cravings):

Options How to use
Gums Chew one piece of gum until the taste becomes strong. Rest the piece of nicotine gum between your gum and cheek and wait for the taste to fade. When the taste has faded, start to chew the gum again. Repeat the process for up to half an hour.
Microtab Place under your tongue and let the tab dissolve over a period of 30 minutes. Do not chew or swallow.
Patchs You should aply your patch on your upper arm, hip, or front or side of your chest (alternate the area each day). Carefully apply the sticky side to your chosen area and press the patch onto your skin with the palm of your hand or fingertips. After 16 or 24 hours of wear (depending on the brand of the product), remove the patch.
